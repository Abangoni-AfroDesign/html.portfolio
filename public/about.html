About Me

Abangoni AfroDesign is an emerging creative tech venture founded by an aspiring Full-Stack Developer and UX/UI Designer. Passionate about crafting innovative user-centered digital solutions, that adapt more quickly to a rapidly changing market.

We aim to blend Afro-inspired design with modern web development and intuitive design that creates scale and agility across markets and media and design that inspires people.

Currently in the learning phase, the founder is horning skills in front-end and back-end technologies, including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, Python, and Node.js, alongside UX/UI principles to create seamless, engaging user experiences.

Abangoni AfroDesign is committed to building culturally vibrant, functional and accessible websites and applications that resonate with cross-cultural design.
